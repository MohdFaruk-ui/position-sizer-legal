Privacy Policy for Position Sizer

Last Updated: May 2026

1. Zero Data Collection Architecture
Position Sizer is built with a strict "Local-First" architecture. We do NOT collect, store, or transmit your personal data, trade logs, or financial information to any external servers. All data, including your trade journal and custom presets, is stored locally on your device using a local database.

2. Analytics and Payments
To provide premium features and maintain the app, we use third-party services that strictly comply with global privacy standards:
- Apple App Store / Google Play: Used to securely process your payments. We do not have access to your credit card information.
- RevenueCat: Used to manage your subscription status anonymously. 

3. Your Control
Since your data lives on your device, you have total control. Deleting the app will permanently erase all your saved trade logs and presets.
